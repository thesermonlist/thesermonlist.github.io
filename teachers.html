About The Teachers

Paul Washer

Perhaps known for having the #1 all-time downloaded sermon on SermonAudio.com (which I have available in the Why Christianity section), Paul Washer is a Reformed/Southern Baptist Calvinist preacher that focuses heavily on Jesus and The Gospel.

Unlike any preacher I've heard, Paul Washer speaks the truth even if it is sometimes difficult to hear. Most of the teachings on this site are given by him and I will likely continue to add more of his teachings.

Wayne Grudem

Commonly known for developing a well known freely-available Christian systematic theology book, Wayne Grudem is amazing at analyzing and explaining the details of Christianity as outlined in The Bible.

Many of the items discussed in his book are things not often discussed, but are foundational to Christianity.

Paris Reidhead

Known for his famous 1965 sermon, Ten Shekels and a Shirt (which I have available in the Thought Provoking section), Paris Reidhead can take an uncommon old testiment Bible passage and make it so impactful and unforgetable.

Bob P. Murphy

Host of the Bob Murphy Show, Bob Murphy is a Christian, libertarian, and Austrian free market economist. He is a favorite among individuals that are interested in these three areas and ways in which they intersect.
